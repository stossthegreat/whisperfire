# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025  
**Company Name:** Beguile AI LTD  

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Data:** Email, username, password.  
- **Usage Data:** Messages you input for analysis, interaction logs, preferences.  
- **Device Data:** IP address, OS, app version.  
- **Payment Data:** Processed securely by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe, Google Play, Apple).  

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- To operate the App and provide services.  
- To personalize AI-generated results.  
- To improve and troubleshoot performance.  
- For security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.  

## 3. Data Storage & Retention
- Data is stored securely on Firebase/Supabase.  
- Message data may be temporarily processed but not permanently stored unless tied to user history.  
- You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.  

## 4. Sharing of Data
We do **not sell** your personal data.  
We may share data with:  
- Service providers (cloud hosting, payment processors).  
- Legal authorities if required by law.  

## 5. Security
We implement reasonable security measures but cannot guarantee 100% protection.

## 6. Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

## 7. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR/CCPA), you may request:  
- Access, correction, deletion of your data.  
- Opt-out of data processing (subject to service limitations).
